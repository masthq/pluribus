Medal of Sacrifice Act
Mast-sponsored legislation creating a presidentially-presented medal honoring law enforcement officers and first responders killed in the line of duty. Inspired by three Palm Beach County sheriff's deputies — Deputy Ralph "Butch" Waller, Deputy Ignacio "Dan" Diaz, and Deputy Luis Paez — killed in November 2024. First medals presented by President Trump in the Oval Office. Passed the House unanimously Feb 2026; awaits Senate.

CAB Act / Harmful Algal Blooms
Mast co-authored the Harmful Algal Bloom and Hypoxia Research and Control Act (HABHRCA) reauthorization — the foundational federal law. He then passed the South Florida Clean Coastal Waters Act (signed into law June 2022) requiring the first-ever federal action plan for Everglades-region algal blooms. Also introduced the Water Protection Act (Feb 2026) targeting nonpoint source pollution.
